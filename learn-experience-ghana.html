Learn and Experience Ghana

Immerse Yourself in Language, Culture, and Adventure

Welcome to the Learn English and Experience Ghana Program!

This unique initiative combines English language learning with cultural immersion in Kumasi, the vibrant cultural capital of Ghana. Whether you're a student, professional, or traveler, this program offers an unforgettable opportunity to enhance your English skills while exploring Ghana's rich heritage, traditions, and natural beauty.

Program Highlights

Cultural Immersion Activities

  • Traditional dance and drumming workshops.
  • Storytelling sessions with local elders.
  • Hands-on craft-making in local villages.
  • Traditional Cooking Classes

Historical Site Visits

  • Guided tours of the Manhyia Palace.
  • Excursions to Lake Bosomtwe and Kejetia Market.
  • Visits to the National Cultural Centre and many more acros the country

Community Engagement

  • Volunteer opportunities in local schools or community projects.
  • Interactive sessions with Ghanaian families and communities.

Excursions and Adventures

  • Trips to wildlife parks and nature reserves.
  • Visits to craft villages to learn about traditional arts.
