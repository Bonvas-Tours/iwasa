About Us

Volunteer in Kumasi, Ghana, to engage in education, healthcare, and more, while immersing in local culture and forging impactful community connections.

Cultural Connection and Community Impact Initiative (Expat Services).

Cultural Connection and Community Impact Initiative (expat services) is a product of the IWaSA(Internship, Work and Study Abroad) department of Bonvas Tours Limited based in Kumasi- Ghana. As a volunteer consultation agency, we are dedicated to providing exceptional experiences that exceed our clients' expectations. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and trust-building is evident in our carefully curated programs and personalised support.

Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive support, facilitating a seamless transition and enabling volunteers to contribute meaningfully to the Sustainable Development Goals in Ghana while immersing themselves in the rich local culture.

Vision

To become a leading global platform for volunteer experiences in Ghana, providing comprehensive support, and authentic local connections that foster meaningful cultural exchange, contribute to community development, and facilitate personal growth for volunteers.

Why Choose Us

Individual Tailored Needs

Our team offers tailored solutions to meet your unique needs, ensuring optimal placements and experiences for volunteers and expatriates. With our customised approach, we guarantee a perfect fit for every individual.

Comprehensive Support Service

Choosing us means opting for comprehensive support at every stage of your journey. We provide pre-departure assistance, in-country support, networking opportunities, ongoing guidance, and post-experience support. We aim to ensure you feel fully prepared, confident, and connected throughout your experience.

Positive Social Impact

Volunteers who become integral members of Ghanaian society establish enduring relationships and leave a lasting legacy of positive change while working towards a sustainable development goal.

Skill Development

Through their experiences, volunteers and expatriates develop valuable skills, such as intercultural communication, problem-solving, adaptability, and leadership, which can enhance their personal and professional growth.

Get Involved in Projects

Tech for Community Empowerment

Volunteers with IT skills can make a significant impact by providing digital literacy training, contributing to local software development, and organizing tech workshops. These initiatives help bridge the digital divide, offering essential skills to underserved communities and creating pathways for innovation. Whether through teaching coding, web design, or app development, volunteers can inspire a new generation of tech enthusiasts and empower businesses with tailored software solutions.
Agricultural Advancement through Volunteering

In the agricultural sector, volunteers can enhance productivity by introducing modern farming techniques and offering business skills training to local farmers. Opportunities also include contributing to research projects on crop improvement and pest control, and assisting farmers in developing marketing strategies or forming cooperatives. By empowering farmers with knowledge and tools, volunteers play a vital role in strengthening agricultural enterprises and improving market access.

Community and Education Support Initiatives

Volunteers can drive meaningful change by collaborating with local leaders to address community needs such as sanitation, infrastructure, and housing. Additionally, educational volunteers can provide classroom assistance, help develop inclusive curricula, and organize extracurricular activities that promote holistic development. With a focus on sustainability and capacity building, volunteers also train community members in leadership, project management, and environmental conservation, ensuring long-lasting impact.

Entrepreneurship and Innovation Hub Support

Volunteers passionate about entrepreneurship can contribute by mentoring startups, guiding entrepreneurs through business planning, and conducting workshops on essential topics like digital marketing and financial management. By facilitating networking events and helping innovation hubs develop valuable resources, volunteers foster an ecosystem that supports small businesses and startups, helping them grow and connect with investors. This involvement not only strengthens local economies but also encourages sustainable entrepreneurial growth.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - Volunteer Experience

  • Volunteers are expected to work from 9 am to 5 pm with some variations according to their place of work. .

  • English is a prerequisite language for volunteers, but knowledge of Twi will be advantageous for fostering deeper connections with the natives in Kumasi. .

  • There is a chef on call for meals. Arrangements can also be made for meals. Adventurous volunteers can also try Ghanaian street food arrayed in Kumasi. .

  • Complementary services such as Twi language lessons, Cultural Immersion activities, local SIM cards, and portable WiFi are provided at a fee. .

  • The main means of transportation is minivan rides, however, Uber and Bolt services may require a fee. .

  • (CCCII) provides assistance for visa applications to the destination country(Ghana) .
